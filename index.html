Scale your Payments globally with IXOPAY

Get in touch

IXOPAY is a best-of-breed, highly scalable and PCI-certified payment orchestration platform for white label clients and enterprise merchants. The modern, easily extendable architecture provides intelligent routing, cascading, and state-of-the-art risk management functions as well as centralized reconciliation and settlements along with plugin-based integration of acquirers and payment service providers.

IXOPAY is the software platform for consolidating and optimizing online payments.
IXOPAY centralizes and maintains more than 200 payment methods, adding up to five new ones each month.
Easily optimize your transaction fees and open up new markets with IXOPAY.
